Can I legally change my name?

Maybe. The California Courts website gives step-by-step instructions for changing your name online at the following website: http://www.courts.ca.gov/selfhelp-namechange.htm, which has specific information for people who are divorcing, getting married, changing their legal gender, and other requests.
