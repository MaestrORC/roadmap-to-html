Which tribes in California offer some form of tribal housing?

The following are some California tribes offering tribal housing through HUD. For the full list of tribes in California that receive HUD support, see https://www.hud.gov/sites/documents/DIRECTORYTRIBE_TDHE.PDF [3390]

    Yurok Indian Housing Authority
    North Fork Rancheria Indian Housing Authority
    Tuolumne Me-Wuk Housing Authority
    All Mission Indian Housing Authority
    Karuk Tribe Housing Authority
    Round Valley Indian Housing Authority
    Northern Circle Indian Housing Authority
    Enterprise Rancheria Indian Housing Authority
    Hoopa Valley Indian Housing Authority
    Modoc Lassen Indian Housing Authority
    Tule River Housing Authority
    Owens Valley Indian Housing Authority
    Chico Rancheria Housing Corporation
    Chukchansi Indian Housing Authority
    Pit River Tribal Housing Board
    Quechan Housing Authority
    Scotts Valley Housing Department
    Susanville Indian Rancheria Housing Authority
  1. 3390

    This list includes the 18 California tribally designated housing entities that were included in the Southwest region of the national directory of tribes and tribally designated housing entities. For a link to this list, see https://www.hud.gov/program_offices/public_indian_housing/ih/codetalk/onap/map/nationalmap/. ↑
