Both a California State ID and a California Driver License serve as an official government-issued, photo identification that can be used to prove your identity, age, and legal presence* in the United States. Either one will allow you to prove your identity, for example, when you open a bank account, register to vote, or apply for jobs, housing, or public benefits.

If you eventually want to drive, you will need to get a Driver License, but because that process requires testing, we recommend that you get a California State ID first, so that you can get an official ID faster. Once you get a CA State ID, you can always go back and take a driving test to get your Driver License. But this allows you to have official ID easily and quickly.
