WHAT NEW INFRACTIONS ARE CREATED UNDER PROP. 64?

The following crimes are now classified as infractions that are punishable by a fine:

    Growing marijuana at home in a space that is visible to the public[3124]
    Using marijuana in public[3125]
    Smoking marijuana in a place where smoking of any kind is prohibited[3126]
    Smoking marijuana within 1,000 feet of a school, daycare, or youth center[3127]
    Open container of marijuana in a vehicle[3128]
    Possession of marijuana on the grounds of a school, daycare, or youth center[3129]
