How much do ABE classes cost?

If you’re currently incarcerated:

In a federal prison,[2755] a California state prison,[2756] or a California county jail,[2757] ABE classes (including necessary books and study materials) are offered at no cost to you.

If you’re formerly incarcerated:

Free or low-cost ABE classes may be available in adult schools or community colleges near you. Many schools provide ABE classes for free, and provide books and materials for free too. Others may charge registration and tuition fees (up to $25-50 per semester or per class) and/or require you to buy your own books and materials. Some schools that charge fees may also offer financial aid to low-income students. You will need to check with the individual school or program about fees and financial aid.[2758]
