What are my options if the DMV told me I have an outstanding traffic ticket?

For parking tickets:

    Once you know where you owe old parking tickets, you should contact the local parking authority directly to find out how much you owe and what to do next.
    If the ticket is old enough, it may have been sent to collections. If this is the case, you will need to contact the collection agency (county collections, a private agency, or the California Franchise Tax Board) to find out how to pay off your debt. (For more information on finding the collection agency that has your parking ticket debt, see PG.¬†743.)
