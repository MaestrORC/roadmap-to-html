Parole & Probation

Being under some type of supervision is usually required for you to be released from prison or jail into community. In the PAROLE & PROBATION CHAPTER, you will learn that in California, there are many types of supervision‚Äîstate parole, county probation, Mandatory Supervision, PRCS, federal probation & federal parole. This chapter will help you understand what it means to be under supervision, what special rules you have to follow, and all of the rights you have as a person in reentry.
