Where are Indian Health Service (IHS) offices located in California?

California has 31 tribal health programs, operating 57 clinics. Additionally, IHS funds eight urban health programs. Look online for the nearest IHS office or health care facility at http://www.ihs.gov/findhealthcare. For a list of California-specific health programs, see www.ihs.gov/california/index.cfm/health-programs/health-programs.
