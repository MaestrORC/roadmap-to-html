I’m homeless. Can I still register to vote in California?

Yes. You don’t need a home address to register. Using the map on the bottom of the voter registration form, you can identify two cross-streets where you usually stay. If you are registering online, you can check the box that says, “I do not have a street address” in the address section.[250]

  1. 250

    Voter Registration Application, Cal. Sec’y of State, http://registertovote.ca.gov/. ↑
